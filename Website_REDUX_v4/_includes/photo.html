Photography.

When I was about 13-years-old, I watched A Clockwork Orange and hated it. I didn't understand why so many people highly regarded that film, so I watched it again (And I feel in love with it). To get that "eye" for photography (like Stanley did) I decided to learn photography. I began my journey in photography when I first attend high school. Since then I have work closely with various high-school journalism conventions (ATPI and SIPA) helping promote photo-journalism and open-source solutions with high-school students. Currently, I work on lighting techniques that can be used in photography and in filmmaking (hitting two birds with one stone).

  • Association of Texas Photography Instructors

    The Association of Texas Photography Instructors (ATPI) provides information and resources to teachers and students that work with photography, whether in Art, Journalism, Industrial Technology or Vocational curriculum areas.

  • Southern Interscholastic Press Association

    March 2007-Present

    The Southern Interscholastic Press Association (SIPA) is a nonprofit organization whose purpose is to promote journalistic professionalism by public schools in the Southeast of the United States of America.
